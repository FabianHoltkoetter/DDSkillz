<skillname>_<questiontype>.json

<skillname>     represents the corresponding skill to be tested.
<questiontype>  is the type of questions that is stored in this file.

Every Skill can have multiple files, depending on how many different types of quesitons he uses.

Available questiontypes:
<multiple>      A question with multiple correct answers.
<single>        A question with a single correct answer.
<yesorno>       Choice between Yes or No.
<value>         A question with a slider to choose the value.
<valuerange>    A question with a slider accepting a range of answers.

Please refer to the dummy-files for further information.
